About Brainiac Games!

Corporate Headquarters
Miami, Florida

Founded in 2022, Braniac Games is an American company founded by Kenny, Jonathan, and Natalie.

The company is headquartered in Florida and has more than 40 offices worldwide. We strive to create a fun, online outlet for those who may not have the means to entertain themselves
